Send large files directly over the Internet.

Instructions

  • If you want to send a file:
    1. On the Transfer Sheet below, choose the file you want to send, field (c), by pressing the "Choose file" button.
    2. Let the other person know your name, found in field (a).
    3. Press "Sign and submit" to start the transfer.

  • If you want to receive a file:
    1. On the Transfer Sheet below, enter the name given to you by the other person in field (b).
    2. Press "Sign and submit" to start the transfer.
